Welcome!

Ma-Korpo story began in 1997 with a clear and unique vision of the future of fast food. Recognizing the importance of healthy living, a love for flavour and authenticity, Ma-korpo creation was by no means accidental.

Since our humble beginnings as a family-run eatery in Monrovia, Ma-Korpo has been able to maintain our individuality and creativity, while growing into a unique healthy african and foreign fast food chain. Our team takes a great pride in offering customers a healthy alternative to be part of Tradition fast food. We hope you enjoy buying from us
